Express

Duration: March 2024 - Current

In this mobile game, users are tasked with strategically forming mathematical expressions within a grid by creating a path of numbers and operators. The objective is to match a specified target number, challenging players to enhance their problem-solving and arithmetic skills. The game is designed with multiple difficulty levels to cater to both beginners and advanced enthusiasts, providing an intellectually stimulating experience. Its professional interface and thoughtful gameplay mechanics make it both educational and engaging for a diverse audience.

This puzzle is still under development and will be available soon. Please check back shortly for updates!

Tumble

Duration: Oct 2023 - Current

This iOS game is a variation of the classic 2048 puzzle, enhanced with a rotating board feature that causes tiles to shift and settle based on gravity. The design includes customizable grids and color schemes, optimizing user experience through a clean and user-friendly interface. The game was developed using MVC architecture, with user interfaces and interactions built using XCode IDE storyboard and UIKit, ensuring functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Check it out on the appstore

Background Noise Suppression

Duration: Aug 2020 - Dec 2020

I conducted extensive research on digital signal processing and machine learning-based speech extraction, focusing on the application of these technologies in enhancing communication clarity. Additionally, I evaluated the use of recurrent neural networks in real-time audio denoising, exploring their potential to significantly improve audio quality by removing background noise. This work not only deepened my expertise in advanced audio processing techniques but also contributed to the development of more efficient and effective noise reduction solutions.

PuzzLED

Duration: Aug 2018 - Dec 2018

I built an interactive puzzle board, equipped with a triangular lattice of LEDs, controlled by an Arduino, which enabled the display of colorful tangrams. This innovative project was recognized for its creativity and educational value, earning the 2018 Children's Museum of Blacksburg Selection award. This puzzle continues to be on display and is still enjoyed by visitors at the Children's Museum of Blacksburg today!
