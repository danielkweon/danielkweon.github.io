01. About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel. I like building systems that scale and puzzle games that make people think. I got into software in college when I realized I could mix math with building stuff people actually use.

So far I've worked at Macro, Amazon, and Bloomberg. At Macro I owned the metadata & properties platform and the Markdown editor, plus task management and AI chat. At Amazon I built the work order system for inventory replenishment. At Bloomberg I worked on fixed income trading billing.

Right now I'm looking for the next thing and spending time on puzzles — building and shipping mobile games to the App Store. When I'm not coding, I'm usually exploring the city, solving puzzles, or over-optimizing something that was probably fine already.
