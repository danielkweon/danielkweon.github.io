02. Where I've Been

Software Engineer @ Macro

July 2024 — January 2026

At Macro, I owned the metadata and properties platform across all workspace entity types, enabling extensibility and unified querying. I designed and implemented a permissions-aware properties service from the ground up (user- and system-defined properties, automated tagging, relationship inference) and drove cross-team alignment on property semantics adopted across multiple product teams.

I built and rolled out a task management system on the properties platform and migrated the company off Linear. It was adopted company-wide and by external customers, scaling quickly in production.

I developed the Markdown editor driving much of in-product activity—real-time collaboration, cross-workspace integrations, agentic AI writing workflows—and improved UX and reliability through telemetry, performance tuning, and bug triage.

I built an AI chat with WebSocket token streaming and context-aware prompt assembly of conversations and documents. I also enabled in-browser docx rendering and editing via LibreOfficeKit (C++ / WebAssembly), significantly reducing document load times.

Software Engineer II @ Amazon
